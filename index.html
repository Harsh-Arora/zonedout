Why Us
Privacy

No one except the client and the therapist is allowed to enter the meeting. Our sessions are purely confidential with the option of our customers choosing to stay anonymous even while booking a session.

Quality

Choose the therapist that is best suited for you! We help you in analysing your issues and connect you with the best in the field!

Convenience

Book a session at your preferable time within the comfort of your home. Change your time slots and days conveniently even after the payment is made.

Affordability

Therapy is now as cheap as eating a Dominos Pizza on a Sunday! Lead a happier life without worrying about the costs!

Mission and Vision

Save the lives of 8 million people who die due to mental illnesses each year! How? By building a community where people freeky speak about anything that’s bothering them without the fear of judgement or stigma! Why wait for something to happen before we start talking about our mental health? Why not make it as common as going to a gym for our physical health everyday! If you are someone who thinks alike, join us today as we bring about a change in the mental health industry!
